About us

Pixa steel for metal industries. founded in the year 1997 and its first brand name was Al-Shimmy company and after the technological developments that occurred in the metal industry, the name was changed to Pixa steel in the year 2018. We are a leading company in the manufacture of metal structures, trusses, overhead and bridge cranes and billboards. we have an expert team that has the skill to develop strategies that suit clients' needs. 24 years of experience and dedication.
